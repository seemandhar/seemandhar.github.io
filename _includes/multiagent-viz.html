How Embodied Agents Discover Robot Skills

A closed-loop robotic coding agent, in the spirit of NVIDIA GEAR's ASPIRE. A manipulator on the sim floor runs a code-as-policy program while the agent loops Execute → Inspect → Repair → Validate over its per-primitive multimodal traces. Each validated repair is distilled into a shared Skill Library — real skills like handover(), open_drawer(), can_in_trash(). Drag to orbit · click a stage to dispatch an actor.

  • 1 · Execute — runs the code-as-policy program on the robot, logging per-primitive traces (perception, planning, grasping).
  • 2 · Inspect — localizes the failing primitive by diagnosing the multimodal traces.
  • 3 · Repair — synthesizes a targeted code-as-policy fix (e.g. a multi-angle approach).
  • 4 · Validate — re-executes closed-loop; a passing repair becomes a reusable skill.
