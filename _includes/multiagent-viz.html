How Multi-Agent Systems Automate Research

A 3D research loop: agent spheres orbit a central knowledge core, passing an artifact through Literature → Hypothesis → Experiment → Validation. Each validated artifact settles into the core as a new discovery. Drag to orbit the scene · click an agent to launch a research thread.

  • 1 · Literature — surveys prior work and extracts open gaps.
  • 2 · Hypothesis — proposes a novel, testable idea.
  • 3 · Experiment — designs and runs experiments for evidence.
  • 4 · Validation — verifies results; passing artifacts become discoveries.
