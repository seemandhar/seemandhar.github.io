How Embodied Agents Discover Robot Skills

A closed-loop robotic coding agent, in the spirit of NVIDIA GEAR's ASPIRE. An actor runs a code-as-policy program on the robot, then loops Execute → Inspect → Repair → Validate over per-primitive multimodal traces. Every validated repair is distilled into a shared Skill Library the whole fleet reuses. Drag to orbit · click a stage to dispatch an actor (candidate program).

  • 1 · Execute — runs the code-as-policy program, logging per-primitive traces (perception, planning, grasping).
  • 2 · Inspect — localizes the failure by diagnosing the multimodal traces.
  • 3 · Repair — synthesizes a targeted code-as-policy fix for the faulty primitive.
  • 4 · Validate — re-executes closed-loop; a passing repair is distilled into a reusable skill.
